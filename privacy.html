Privacy Policy

Effective March 27, 2026

This policy explains what information hone collects, how we use it, and your rights. We built hone to help you track your improvement over time — not to harvest your data. We keep things simple.

What We Collect

When you use hone, we collect only what's needed to make the app work:

  • Account info — Your email address and display name, provided when you sign up with email/password or Sign in with Apple.
  • Your content — The photos and video clips you record in the app. These are uploaded to our servers so we can store them and generate your reveal/timelapse videos.
  • Purchase info — If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple and RevenueCat. We receive a record that you unlocked the feature, but we never see your payment details.

That's it. We don't collect your location, contacts, browsing history, or device identifiers for advertising. There are no third-party analytics or tracking tools in the production app. No ads, no ad networks.

Sign in with Apple

If you use Sign in with Apple, Apple may provide us with your name and email address (or a private relay email, if you choose to hide your real one). We use this only to create and manage your account.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for one purpose: making hone work for you.

  • Storing and displaying your photos and video clips
  • Generating reveal and timelapse videos from your content
  • Managing your account and purchase status

Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for marketing or advertising. The only external services that interact with your data are:

  • Apple — For Sign in with Apple authentication and App Store purchase processing.
  • RevenueCat — For managing in-app purchase status.
  • Hosting/infrastructure providers — Our servers store your content on our behalf.

Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and content from our servers within 30 days.

Deleting Your Account

You can delete your account directly within the app under Settings. This removes your profile, all uploaded photos and videos, and any associated data.

Your Rights

California (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, request its deletion, and opt out of its sale. We don't sell your data.

EU/UK (GDPR): You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, restrict or object to processing, and data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, email us.

Children

hone is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make meaningful changes, we'll update the effective date at the top. Continued use of hone after changes means you accept the updated policy.
